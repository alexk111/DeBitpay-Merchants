DeBitpay Merchants

There are merchants using Bitpay to accept Bitcoin payments, but they are not aware of issues related to the payment protocol Bitpay forces everyone to use. Please spend a few minutes to draw their attention to it.

What to do?

  • If you know a merchant who uses Bitpay to accept Bitcoin payments, post a tweet mentioning their @TwitterId and put the hashtag #DeBitpay (Copy Hashtag).
  • Monitor twitter feed with the hashtag #DeBitpay from time to time and join the conversations.

Some ideas for tweets:

  • Ask for alternative merchants accepting Bitcoin without Bitpay's payment protocol.
  • Ask the merchant to switch to another payment processor, and send the list of alternatives (Copy Link).
